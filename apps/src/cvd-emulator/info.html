Check Images for Color Constraints

Reto Stauffer, Claus O. Wilke, Achim Zeileis

A simple app that allows you to check an existing jpg/png image for (possible) color constraints. The image will be converted to protanope vision, deuteranope vision, and a desaturated version (monochromatic vision). Allows a rapid check whether the colors used in the image show some constraints with respect to color vision deficiency or color blindness.

Note that even though you "upload" the image the app runs entirely in your browser and the image never leaves your computer.
